Jordan Argrett, (he/him/his) Program Director

Jordan Argrett is a third year PhD student at the Odum School of Ecology. His research focuses on parasitic plants and their impact on natural communities above and below ground. Outside of research, Jordan creates instrumental hip-hop and shoots film photography. You can contact Jordan at jordan.argrett@uga.edu.

  • Learn more

Summer Blanco, (she/they) Vice President of Events

Summer is a third year PhD student in the Department of Plant Biology, co-advised by Drs. Shu-Mei Chang & Jim Leebens-Mack. They are interested in how flower color changes in response to the environment. Outside of school, Summer loves to roller skate (weekly at Athens Skate Inn) and sing karaoke! You can contact Summer at Summer.Blanco@uga.edu.

Katie Dillon, (she/her) Vice President of Digital Media

Katie is a third year PhD candidate in the Institute of Bioinformatics at UGA. Her research is focused on European ticks and the impact of tick genomics. Outside of research, she enjoys making yarn-based crafts, playing indie video games, and taking afternoon naps. You can contact Katie at Katie.Dillon@uga.edu.
